Prayer for Acceptance of Death

O Jesus, adoring your last breath, I beseech you to receive my own. I know not whether I shall have free use of my understanding when I leave this world. So from this moment I offer to you my agony and all the pains of my death. You are my Father and Savior. I commend my souls into your hands. I desire that my last moment be united to your dying breath and that the last beat of my heart be an act of pure love of you.

My Lord God, from this day I accept from your hands, with resignation and cheerfulness the kind of death it may please you to send me, with all its sorrows, pains and anguish. Amen
