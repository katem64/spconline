## â“ Common Questions

**Q: What if I forget to pray one day?**
A: Your total days are never lost! Only your consecutive streak resets. Or turn off streaks in Settings to remove that pressure.

**Q: Can others see my prayer history?**
A: No! Everything is stored only on your device. It's completely private between you and God.

**Q: What if I get a new phone?**
A: Each device tracks independently. Your prayer history doesn't transfer, but you start fresh on the new device.

**Q: Can I delete my history?**
A: Yes! Clear your browser data or reinstall the app to start fresh. (Note: This will delete all history permanently)

**Q: Does it use my data/battery?**
A: Very minimal! The tracker is lightweight and works offline, so no data usage after initial install.

**Q: I don't see any statistics yet?**
A: Start by visiting a few prayer pages over several days. Statistics will build up as you pray!
