Prayer for the Dying

Lord, Have mercy.
Christ, have mercy.
Lord, have mercy.

Our Father . . . Hail Mary . . .

O Lord Jesus Christ, who died for us on the cross, deign, I implore you, to offer up and show to God, the Father almighty, on behalf of your servant, ___. The bitter sufferings and pains you endured on the cross for us, particularly in that hour when you commend your spirit into the hands of Father. May she/he be filled with your peace, and surrender himself/herself totally into your hands, knowing that you have loved him/her with an everlasting love, You who with the Father and the Holy Spirit live and reign forever and ever. Amen. Into your hands, O Lord, I commend my Spirit! Lord Jesus Christ, receive my spirit.

Those who should pronounce the name of JESUS aloud and say the following in the ear:

Into your hands, O Lord, I commend my Spirit! Lord Jesus Christ, receive my spirit. Holy Mary, pray for me. Mary, Mother of grace, Mother of mercy, protect me from the enemy and receive my soul at the hour of my death. St. Joseph, pray for me.

Jesus, Mary and Joseph, I give you my heart and my soul! Jesus, Mary and Joseph, assist me in my last agony! Jesus, Mary and Joseph, in your blessed company may I sleep and rest in peace.
