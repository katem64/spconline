The Mysteries of Light

The Baptism of Jesus in the Jordan River

“And a voice spoke from heaven. ‘This is my Son, the Beloved, my favor rests on him’.” (Mt 3:17) Scripture Reading: Mt. 3:13-17 or Mk 1:9-11 or Lk 3:21-22

The First Miracle Performed by Jesus at a Wedding in Cana

“His mother said to the servants, ‘Do whatever he tells you’.” (Jn 2:5) Scripture Reading: jn 2:1-11

The Proclamation of God’s Kingdom

“He went round the whole of Galilee teaching in their synagogues proclaiming the good News of the Kingdom and curing all kinds of diseases and sickness among the people.” (Mt 4:23) Scripture Reading: mt 4:13-17, 23-25 or Mt 5:1-12 or Mk 1:14-15, 21-28 or Lk 4:14-22a

The Transfiguration of Jesus on Mount Tabor

“There in their presence he was transfigured: his face shone like the sun and his clothes became as white as the light.” (Mt 17:2) Scripture Reading: Mt. 17:1-8 or Lk 9:28b-35 or Mk 9:2-8

The Institution of the Eucharist

“Until the Lord comes, therefore, every time you eat this brad and drink this cup, you are proclaiming his death . . .” (1 Cor 11:26) Scripture Reading: Lk 22:14-20 or Mt. 26:26-28 or Mk 14:22-24 or 1Cor 11:23-26
