Regina Coeli

V: Queen of heaven, rejoice. Alleluia

R: The Son whom it was your privilege to bear. Alleluia

V: As risen as He said. Alleluia

R: Pray God for us. Alleluia

V: Rejoice and be glad, Virgin Mary. Alleluia

R: For the Lord is truly risen. Alleluia

Let us pray: O god, you were pleased to give joy to the world through the resurrection of your Son, our Lord Jesus Christ. Grant we beseech you that through the meditation of the Virgin Mary His Mother, we may come to possess the joys of life everlasting. Through the same Christ our Lord. Amen
